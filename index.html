Be first – be featured!

Make sure to create and upload your quality music to FREQS. We recommend releasing 3-4 tracks a month to ensure constant exposure of your music. It will also increase your chances to star on FREQS' exclusive top-10 page often and take part in our special promotions!

Try giving your page on FREQS a unique look, with your own special touch. We encourage our artists to include a photograph of themselves. It is highly recommended to share your music by creating additional pages on YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, and other social networks. We strongly suggest to share them often and so increase the exposure to your musical creations. Artists who release more – sell more!
